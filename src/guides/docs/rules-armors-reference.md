# Armors Reference

This module provides complete reference tables for all armor and equipment available in MEST Tactics QSR.

## Basic Armors List

Characters are allowed **just one of each Type** of armor listed (one Suit, one Helm, one Shield, one Gear).

**Note:** Be sure to adjust **REF** and **Agility** per the **[Laden]** trait.

| Armor | Type | AR | Traits | BP |
|-------|------|-----|--------|-----|
| **Helmet** | Helm | - | Protective. | 3 |
| **Full Helm** | Helm | 1 | [Blinders]. Protective. | 5 |
| **Shield, Small** | Shield | - | [1H]. Coverage. Deflect. | 8 |
| **Shield, Light** | Shield | 1 | [1H]. Coverage. | 8 |
| **Shield, Medium** | Shield | 1 | [1H][Laden]. Coverage. Deflect. | 10 |
| **Armored Gear** | Gear | - | Conceal. Deflect. | 5 |
| **Armor, Light** | Suit | 2 | [Laden]. Deflect. | 8 |
| **Armor, Medium** | Suit | 4 | [Laden 2]. Deflect. | 13 |
| **Armor, Heavy** | Suit | 6 | [Laden 3][Lumbering]. Deflect. | 15 |

**Notes:**
- **AR** = Armor Rating (reduces Wound damage)
- **Traits** = Special rules (see [[rules-traits-list|Rules: Traits List]])
- **BP** = Build Point cost
- **Type** = Armor slot (only one of each type allowed per character)

### Special Rules

**Armor, Heavy:**
> This is an **uncommon armor type** because it is expensive to craft and fit to any character. Unless players agree: **limit to 1 per 2 models each Side**.

## Armor Type Reference

### Helm (Head Protection)

| Armor | AR | Traits | BP | Notes |
|-------|-----|--------|-----|-------|
| **Helmet** | - | Protective. | 3 | Basic head protection |
| **Full Helm** | 1 | [Blinders]. Protective. | 5 | Full face coverage; -1m in Scrum |

**Helm Rules:**
- Only **one Helm** per character
- **Protective:** Treat critical hits as normal hits
- **[Blinders]** (Full Helm only): -1m in Scrum, no Bow attacks, Thrown -1m

### Shield (Hand-Held Defense)

| Armor | AR | Traits | BP | Hand | Notes |
|-------|-----|--------|-----|------|-------|
| **Shield, Small** | - | [1H]. Coverage. Deflect. | 8 | 1H | No AR, but provides Deflect |
| **Shield, Light** | 1 | [1H]. Coverage. | 8 | 1H | AR 1, no Deflect |
| **Shield, Medium** | 1 | [1H][Laden]. Coverage. Deflect. | 10 | 1H | AR 1 + Deflect, -1 MOV |

**Shield Rules:**
- Only **one Shield** per character
- Requires **[1H]** (one hand)
- **Coverage:** Block attacks from any direction
- **Deflect:** +1b Defender Hit Test vs Range Combat
- **[Laden]:** -1 MOV (Medium shield only)

### Suit (Body Armor)

| Armor | AR | Traits | BP | Burden | Notes |
|-------|-----|--------|-----|--------|-------|
| **Armor, Light** | 2 | [Laden]. Deflect. | 8 | -1 MOV | Minimal protection |
| **Armor, Medium** | 4 | [Laden 2]. Deflect. | 13 | -2 MOV | Balanced protection |
| **Armor, Heavy** | 6 | [Laden 3][Lumbering]. Deflect. | 15 | -3 MOV, No climb/jump | Maximum protection |

**Suit Rules:**
- Only **one Suit** per character
- **Deflect:** +1b Defender Hit Test vs Range Combat
- **[Laden X]:** -X MOV, -X REF/CCA (unless Attentive Ordered)
- **[Lumbering]** (Heavy only): Flanked/Cornered/Confined = -1b each

### Gear (Accessory Armor)

| Armor | AR | Traits | BP | Notes |
|-------|-----|--------|-----|-------|
| **Armored Gear** | - | Conceal. Deflect. | 5 | Bracers, greaves, padding |

**Gear Rules:**
- Only **one Gear** per character
- **Conceal:** Does not count against WYSIWYG
- **Deflect:** +1b Defender Hit Test vs Range Combat

## Equipment List

All Equipment have the **Conceal** trait. Allow each character **at most one Item** listed.

| Equipment | Type | Traits | BP |
|-----------|------|--------|-----|
| **Medicinal, Alcohol** | Advantage | Grit. | 17 |
| **Medicinal, Stimulant** | Advantage | Brawn. | 11 |

**Notes:**
- **Type: Advantage** = Provides temporary benefit when used
- **Conceal** = Does not count against WYSIWYG; one per character automatically
- **Grit** = Fear immunity, ignore first Wound once per game
- **Brawn** = +1 STR (except Close Combat Damage)

### Equipment Usage

**Medicinal, Alcohol:**
- **Effect:** Gain **Grit** trait for duration
- **Duration:** Typically one scene or mission
- **Side Effects:** May impose penalties (GM discretion)

**Medicinal, Stimulant:**
- **Effect:** Gain **Brawn** trait for duration
- **Duration:** Typically one scene or mission
- **Side Effects:** May impose penalties (GM discretion)

## Armor by Genre

### Bronze Age (~2250 BCE)
**Available:** Helmet, Shield Small/Light, Armor Light (hide/linen)
**Typical Loadouts:**
- **Light Infantry:** Helmet + Shield Light (11 BP)
- **Heavy Infantry:** Helmet + Shield Medium + Armor Light (21 BP)
- **Champion:** Full set with Armor Medium (34 BP)

### Medieval (~500-1500 CE)
**Available:** All basic armors
**Typical Loadouts:**
- **Peasant:** No armor or Shield Small only (8 BP)
- **Man-at-Arms:** Full Helm + Armor Medium + Shield Medium (28 BP)
- **Knight:** Full set with Armor Heavy (41 BP)

### Renaissance (~1300-1700 CE)
**Available:** All basic armors
**Typical Loadouts:**
- **Duelist:** Helmet + Armor Medium (18 BP)
- **Pikeman:** Full Helm + Armor Medium + Shield Small (24 BP)
- **Musketeer:** Helmet + Armor Light (11 BP)

### Modern (~1900-2025 CE)
**Available:** Helmets, Gear (ballistic vests as Armor)
**Typical Loadouts:**
- **Police:** Helmet + Armored Gear (8 BP)
- **Soldier:** Full Helm + Armor Medium (ballistic vest) (18 BP)

### Science Fiction / Fantasy (~2025+)
**Available:** All armors, possibly enhanced with materials (see [[rules-armor-materials|Rules: Armor Materials]])
**Typical Loadouts:**
- **Scout:** Helmet + Armor Light + Armored Gear (16 BP)
- **Soldier:** Full Helm + Armor Medium + Shield (28+ BP)
- **Tank:** Full set with Armor Heavy (41+ BP)

## Armor Material Adjustments

For games using different technological levels, apply material modifiers from [[rules-armor-materials|Rules: Armor Materials]]:

| Era | AR Modifier | Example |
|-----|-------------|---------|
| **Ancient/Medieval** | -2 | Iron armor |
| **Renaissance** | -1 | Steel plate |
| **Industrial** | 0 | Dense steel |
| **Modern** | +1 to +2 | Ceramic plates |
| **Future** | +3 to +7 | Graphene polymers |

**Example: Iron Armor, Medium**
- Base: Armor, Medium (AR 4, 13 BP)
- Material: Iron (-2 AR)
- **Result:** AR 2, 11 BP

## Quick Reference: Armor Traits

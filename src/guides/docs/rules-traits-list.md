# Traits List

This module provides a definitive list and description for all traits available in MEST Tactics. Traits are special rules that modify a character's or item's capabilities.

## Ability Traits (Beneficial)

*   **Archery:** The character is trained in the rapid use of bows. This trait allows the character to ignore the [Reload] trait on Bow weapons. Does not provide the Shoot trait.
*   **Armor X:** Provides an Armor Rating (AR) equal to X. Each point of AR reduces incoming Wound damage by one. This trait is inherent to items of the Armor type.
*   **Charge:** The character gains +1m on their Attacker Hit Test if they move into base-contact with the target over Clear terrain, having started from a Free position at least their base-diameter away.
*   **Cleave:** After a successful Close Combat Attack that results in the target being KO'd or Eliminated, the Attacker may immediately perform another Close Combat Attack against a different target within Melee Range. This second attack does not cost AP.
*   **Conceal:** This item can be carried by a character without it being physically represented on the model. A character can have one concealed weapon. Equipment is given Conceal by default.
*   **Counter-strike!:** A character with this trait may perform a Close Combat Attack as a React against an Opponent who makes a Close Combat Attack against them and misses. This React costs 1 AP.
*   **Coverage:** Provides protection from a wide arc. An item with this trait (like a Shield) can be used to block attacks from any direction, negating flanking bonuses related to the item.
*   **Deflect:** This item provides a bonus to the wearer's defense. The character gains +1b to their Defender Hit Test against Range Combat Attacks.
*   **Discrete:** This weapon is small and easily hidden. It can always be considered to have the **Conceal** trait. A character can have any number of Discrete weapons regardless of WYSIWYG limitations.
*   **Fight:** The character is a master of close combat. The character gains +1m to all Close Combat Hit Tests.
*   **Grit:** The character has a strong will to survive. Once per game, when this character would receive a Wound token, they may ignore it. Also provides immunity to Fear and exemptions from Morale tests.
*   **Parry:** After an Opponent resolves a Close Combat Attack against this character, if the character is not KO'd, they may push the attacker back 1". The attacker must move 1" directly away from the character.
*   **Perimeter:** The character is adept at controlling the space around them. Opposing models wishing to move within 1" of this character must spend 1 additional inch of movement for every inch moved within this zone.
*   **Protective:** This item is designed to absorb impacts to a vital area. When a character wearing this item suffers a critical hit (an effect that would cause extra damage), they may treat it as a normal hit instead.
*   **Reach:** This weapon has a long haft or blade, allowing the wielder to attack from a safer distance. The character can make Close Combat Attacks against targets up to 1" away, and does not need to be in base-contact.
*   **Reload:** This weapon requires time to reload after being fired. It cannot be fired two actions in a row. The character must perform a Fiddle action (costing 1 AP) to reload the weapon before it can be fired again.
*   **Shoot:** The character is a master of ranged combat. The character gains +1m to all Range Combat Hit Tests.
*   **Stun:** A successful hit with this weapon can daze the target. If an attack with this weapon causes at least one Wound, the target also receives a Delay token.
*   **Throwable:** This Melee weapon is balanced for throwing. It can be used to make a Range Combat Attack with an Optimal Range (OR) equal to the character's STR attribute in inches. It has the [Discard!] trait when thrown.

## Disability Traits (Penalties)

*   **[1H]:** This item requires one hand to use effectively.
*   **[2H]:** This item requires two hands to use effectively. A character may use a [2H] item with only one hand, but they suffer a -1b penalty on all Tests made with that item.
*   **[Burst]:** This weapon fires in bursts. When attacking, the weapon uses the ROF trait with burst fire rules.
*   **[Discard!]:** This item is lost after one use. Remove it from the character's sheet after it has been used.
*   **[Feed]:** This weapon requires careful feeding of ammunition. Part of the ROF trait family.
*   **[Jam]:** This weapon may jam when fired. Part of the ROF trait family.
*   **[Jitter]:** This weapon has recoil or instability. Part of the ROF trait family.
*   **[Laden X]:** This item is heavy or cumbersome. The character's MOV is reduced by X inches. If X is not specified, it is 1. Multiple instances of Laden are cumulative.
*   **[Lumbering]:** This armor is exceptionally heavy and rigid. The character cannot perform Move actions that include climbing or jumping. Their Agility value is always considered 0.
*   **[Reload]:** This weapon must be reloaded after firing. The character must spend 1 AP to reload before firing again.
*   **[ROF]:** Rate of Fire. This weapon fires multiple times. Usually paired with {[Jitter, Feed, Jam]. Burst} to indicate firing behavior.
*   **[Stub]:** This weapon is very short. The character suffers a -1m penalty on their Attacker Hit Test when making a Close Combat Attack against an opponent with a weapon that has the **Reach** trait.

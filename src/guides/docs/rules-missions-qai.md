# QAI Missions

## QAI Mission 1: Elimination

Two forces meet on the battlefield. Destroy your opponent's fighting strength before they destroy yours.

**Pre-game**
Use the standard rules. There are just 2 Sides.

**Models per Side / BP / Battlefield / End-game**
- Small: 4 to 8, 250 to 500 BP, 24 x 24 MU, Turn 4
- Medium: 6 to 12, 500 to 750 BP, 36 x 36 MU, Turn 6
- Large: 8 to 16, 750 to 1000 BP, 48 x 48 MU, Turn 8

**Battlefield**
- Terrain.
- Set up the Battlefield using the rules for Environments and Terrain.
- No special terrain requirements.

**Deployment**
- Use standard opposing edge deployment.
- No Hidden starts unless behind Cover and out of LOS.
- Models must deploy within 6 MU of their battlefield edge.

**Special Rules**
- None.

**Game Length**
- Use the standard game length rules.

**Keys to Victory**
- Elimination: +1 VP to Side with highest total BP value of KO'd and Eliminated Opposing models at game end.
- Bottled: +1 VP if Opposing Side fails Bottle Test or has no Ordered models remaining at game end.
- Outnumbered: +1 VP to Side outnumbered 3:2 models or greater at game start; +2 VP if outnumbered 2:1 or greater (only if multiple Sides remain at game end).

## QAI Mission 12: Convergence

Combat erupts as multiple forces converge on contested objectives from all directions. Reinforcements arrive in waves. Timing and positioning decide victory.

**Pre-game**
Use the standard rules. There can be 2 to 4 mutually Opposing Sides.

**Models per Side / BP / Battlefield / End-game**
- Small: 4 to 8, 250 to 500 BP, 24 x 24 MU, Turn 4
- Medium: 6 to 12, 500 to 750 BP, 36 x 36 MU, Turn 6
- Large: 8 to 16, 750 to 1000 BP, 48 x 48 MU, Turn 8

**Battlefield**
- Terrain.
- Set up the Battlefield using the rules for Environments and Terrain.
- Place Control Points (POI terrain pieces) at battlefield center:
  - Small: 2 POIs placed 6 MU apart.
  - Medium: 3 POIs in triangular formation, 8 MU apart.
  - Large: 4 POIs in diamond formation, 10 MU apart.
- Each POI must have at least one terrain element providing Cover within 4 MU.
- Divide battlefield perimeter into equal Entry Edges (one per Side).

**Deployment**
- Each Side divides their Assembly into three Reinforcement Groups of equal BP value (rounding as needed).
- Group A must contain at least one model with Leap, Sprint, Flight, or Glide if available in the Assembly.
- Each Side places one d6 as a Reinforcement die at their Entry Edge before Turn 1.

**Special Rules**
- Reinforcements: At Start of each Turn, each Side rolls their Reinforcement dice. Each die showing 4+ deploys one Reinforcement Group within 6 MU of their Entry Edge. Groups deploy in order: Group A first, then B, then C. At End of each Turn with remaining Groups, place one additional d6 at any unoccupied position along their Entry Edge.
- Dominance: At End of each Turn, each Side controlling a POI (at least 1 model within 4 MU, no Opposing models within 4 MU) awards +1 VP. First Side to accumulate Small 3 VP, Medium 4 VP, Large 5 VP wins immediately.
- No model may target an Opposing model that is Engaged with a third Side's model unless that model attacked them this Turn.
- When a Side is Eliminated, all its controlled POIs become Neutral. Each remaining Side receives +1 VP when a third Side is Eliminated before Turn 3 (Small), Turn 4 (Medium), or Turn 5 (Large).

**Game Length**
- Use the standard game length rules.

**Keys to Victory**
- Dominance: Immediate victory upon reaching VP threshold.
- Elimination: +1 VP for most BP eliminated (tiebreaker only).
- Bottled: +1 VP if Opposing Side fails Bottle Test (tiebreaker only).
- First Blood: +1 VP to first Side to Wound an Opposing model.

## QAI Mission 13: Assault

Infiltrate the facility and destroy its critical systems before reinforcements arrive.

**Pre-game**
Use the standard rules. There are just 2 Sides.

**Models per Side / BP / Battlefield / End-game**
- Small: 4 to 8, Att 375 / Def 250 BP, 24 x 24 MU, Turn 4
- Medium: 6 to 12, Att 750 / Def 500 BP, 36 x 36 MU, Turn 6
- Large: 8 to 16, Att 1000 / Def 750 BP, 48 x 48 MU, Turn 8

**Battlefield**
- Terrain.
- Set up the Battlefield using the rules for Environments and Terrain.
- Place Target Points (SPs), each a POI terrain piece:
  - Small: 1 SP placed 8 MU from Defender edge.
  - Medium: 2 SPs placed 12 MU from Defender edge, 12 MU apart.
  - Large: 3 SPs in triangular formation, 16 MU from Defender edge, 16 MU apart.
- Each SP must have at least one terrain element providing Cover within 4 MU.
- No SP within 6 MU of any battlefield edge.

**Deployment**
- Mission Defender (Garrison) deploys first within 8 MU of their edge.
- All models may start in Wait status at no AP cost.
- Models within 6 MU of any SP gain +1 Wild die Defender Hit Tests.
- Mission Attacker (Saboteurs) deploys no models at game start. All models begin in Reserve status.
- Before Turn 1 begins, place two d6 as Reinforcement dice for Attacker.
- At Start of each Turn, roll all Reinforcement dice. Each die showing 4+ deploys models within 6 MU of any edge except Defender's edge.
- Deployed models may start Hidden if out of LOS of all Opposing models or behind Cover.

**Special Rules**
- Sabotage Action: A Free Attacker model in base-contact with an SP spends 2 AP Fiddle action and passes Unopposed INT Test (DR 2) to destroy the SP. On success, SP is removed and awards +2 VP immediately. Model performing sabotage acquires 1 Delay token. Only one model per Turn may attempt sabotage on a given SP.
- Defender Reinforcements: At End of Turn 2 (Small) / Turn 3 (Medium) / Turn 4 (Large), Defender receives reinforcement:
  - Small: +75 BP deployed within 6 MU of edge, arrives Distracted.
  - Medium: +150 BP deployed within 8 MU of edge, arrives Distracted.
  - Large: +250 BP deployed within 10 MU of edge, arrives Distracted.
- Time Pressure: If no SP destroyed by End of Turn 3 (Small) / Turn 4 (Medium) / Turn 5 (Large), game ends immediately after that Turn.

**Game Length**
- Use the standard game length rules.

**Keys to Victory**
- Sabotage: +2 VP per destroyed SP.
- Elimination: +1 VP to Side with most BP worth of KO'd and Eliminated models.
- POI: +1 VP to Side controlling majority of remaining SPs at game end.
- Bottled: +1 VP if Opposing Side fails Bottle Test or has no Ordered models.

## QAI Mission 14: Dominion

Both Sides race to activate and hold transmission beacons that call in orbital support. The first to achieve sustained signal dominance wins.

**Pre-game**
Use the standard rules. There are just 2 Sides.

**Models per Side / BP / Battlefield / End-game**
- Small: 4 to 8, 250 to 500 BP, 24 x 24 MU, Turn 4
- Medium: 6 to 12, 500 to 750 BP, 36 x 36 MU, Turn 6
- Large: 8 to 16, 750 to 1000 BP, 48 x 48 MU, Turn 8

**Battlefield**
- Terrain.
- Set up the Battlefield using the rules for Environments and Terrain.
- Place Beacon Zones (6 MU diameter circles):
  - Small: 2 Beacons placed 12 MU apart centered on battlefield.
  - Medium: 3 Beacons in triangular formation, 12 MU from center point.
  - Large: 4 Beacons in diamond formation, 16 MU from center point.
- Minimum 8 MU separation between any two Beacons. No terrain requirements within zones.

**Deployment**
- Use standard opposing edge deployment.
- No Hidden starts.
- No model may deploy within 6 MU of any Beacon Zone center.

**Special Rules**
- Dominance: A Beacon Zone is Controlled by a Side when at least 1 Friendly model is within zone boundary and no Opposing models are within zone boundary. At End of each Turn, each Controlled Beacon awards +1 VP to controlling Side. First Side to accumulate Small 3 VP, Medium 4 VP, Large 5 VP wins immediately.
- Courier: Each Side designates one model as Courier (must be Average archetype or lower BP value). Courier gains +1 MOV for this mission. Courier reaching Opposing battlefield edge awards +1 VP immediately, then +1 VP at End of each subsequent Turn it remains on battlefield. If Courier is Eliminated, all VP from Courier is immediately revoked.
- Sanctuary: Each Side's deployment edge is an 8 MU deep Sanctuary Zone. Models within Sanctuary receive +1 Wild die Defender Tests but cannot perform Attack actions. Each full Turn a Side maintains at least 25 percent of starting BP within Sanctuary awards +1 VP at End of Turn.

**Game Length**
- Use the standard game length rules.

**Keys to Victory**
- Dominance: Immediate victory upon reaching VP threshold.
- Courier: VP from edge contact and sustained presence.
- Sanctuary: VP from maintaining reserve force.
- Elimination: +1 VP for most BP eliminated (tiebreaker only).
- Bottled: +1 VP if Opponent fails Bottle Test (tiebreaker only).

## QAI Mission 15: Recovery

Scattered intelligence caches contain vital data. Extract them before the opposition, but each extraction triggers security protocols that escalate the battlefield's danger.

**Pre-game**
Use the standard rules. There are just 2 Sides.

**Models per Side / BP / Battlefield / End-game**
- Small: 4 to 8, 250 to 500 BP, 24 x 24 MU, Turn 4
- Medium: 6 to 12, 500 to 750 BP, 36 x 36 MU, Turn 6
- Large: 8 to 16, 750 to 1000 BP, 48 x 48 MU, Turn 8

**Battlefield**
- Terrain.
- Set up the Battlefield using the rules for Environments and Terrain.
- Place Vital Assets (ICs), each a Small OM:
  - Small: 3 ICs placed randomly (roll 2d6 x 2 MU from corner), min 8 MU apart.
  - Medium: 5 ICs placed randomly (roll 2d6 x 3 MU from corner), min 8 MU apart.
  - Large: 7 ICs placed randomly (roll 2d6 x 4 MU from corner), min 8 MU apart.
- Minimum 6 MU from any battlefield edge.
- Roll 1d6 per IC after placement; on 4+ IC begins Hidden (revealed when model comes within 4 MU).
- At least 70 percent battlefield coverage must be Cover-providing terrain.

**Deployment**
- Use standard opposing edge deployment.
- No Hidden starts.
- No model may deploy within 8 MU of any IC location.

**Special Rules**
- Harvest: Acquire IC: 1 AP Fiddle action while Free and in base-contact. Extract IC: 1 AP while in base-contact with any battlefield edge. IC removed from play and awards +1 VP immediately. Extracting model acquires 1 Delay token. Each extraction increases Security Level by 1.
- Catalyst: Security Level effects:
  - Level 2+: All models suffer -1 Base die Range Combat Hit Tests.
  - Level 4+: All models suffer -1 Base die Close Combat Hit Tests.
  - Level 6+ (Small) / Level 7+ (Medium) / Level 8+ (Large): Hard Cover within 8 MU of any IC location degrades to Soft Cover.
- Game ends immediately when one Side extracts:
  - Small: 3 ICs
  - Medium: 4 ICs
  - Large: 5 ICs
- VIP: Each Side designates one model as Analyst (at least 30 BP). Once per game, while Attentive Ordered and carrying an IC, spend 2 AP to immediately extract that IC without exiting battlefield (awards +1 VP, no Delay token). If Analyst is KO'd or Eliminated while carrying ICs, all carried ICs drop as Neutral OMs.
- Sanctuary: Each Side's deployment edge is a 6 MU deep Sanctuary Zone. Models within Sanctuary receive +1 Wild die Defender Tests but cannot perform Attack actions. Each full Turn a Side maintains at least 25 percent of BP within Sanctuary awards +1 VP at End of Turn.

**Game Length**
- Use the standard game length rules.

**Keys to Victory**
- Harvest: Immediate victory upon extracting required ICs.
- Sanctuary: +1 VP per Turn for maintaining reserve force.
- Catalyst: +1 VP to Side that triggered maximum Security Level.
- Elimination: +1 VP for most BP eliminated (tiebreaker only).

## QAI Mission 16: Escort

Extract the VIP before enemy reinforcements seal the perimeter. Every moment counts, but rushing invites disaster.

**Pre-game**
Use the standard rules. There are just 2 Sides.

**Models per Side / BP / Battlefield / End-game**
- Small: 4 to 8, 250 to 500 BP, 24 x 24 MU, Turn 4
- Medium: 6 to 12, 500 to 750 BP, 36 x 36 MU, Turn 6
- Large: 8 to 16, 750 to 1000 BP, 48 x 48 MU, Turn 8

**Battlefield**
- Terrain.
- Set up the Battlefield using the rules for Environments and Terrain.
- Place one Extraction Zone (circle) at Mission Defender's battlefield edge:
  - Small: 3 MU radius.
  - Medium: 4 MU radius.
  - Large: 5 MU radius.
- Place at least one terrain element providing Cover within the Extraction Zone.
- Place the VIP (Elite archetype model, 129 BP) at battlefield center:
  - Small: 8 MU from Extraction Zone center.
  - Medium: 12 MU from Extraction Zone center.
  - Large: 16 MU from Extraction Zone center.
- No terrain within 4 MU of VIP starting position.

**Deployment**
- Mission Defender (Containment Force) deploys first within 8 MU of their edge.
- All models may start in Wait status at no AP cost.
- Models within 6 MU of Extraction Zone gain +1 Wild die Defender Hit Tests.
- No model may deploy within 4 MU of another.
- Mission Attacker (Extraction Team) deploys second on opposite battlefield edge.
- VIP must deploy within 4 MU of a terrain element.
- No model may deploy within 8 MU of Extraction Zone center.

**Special Rules**
- Courier: VIP entering Extraction Zone awards +1 VP immediately. At End of each subsequent Turn VIP remains within zone, award +1 VP. VIP surviving within zone until Small Turn 3, Medium Turn 4, Large Turn 5 grants immediate Attacker victory. VIP within zone cannot perform Attack actions and acquires 1 Delay token upon entry.
- VIP Ability: VIP possesses one ability (chosen at deployment):
  - Decoy: Once per game, place Neutral model within 4 MU; Opposing models must target Decoy first until eliminated.
  - Overdrive: Once per game, +2 MOV this Turn (ignores terrain penalties).
  - Scramble: Once per game, all Opposing models within 8 MU lose Wait status and acquire 1 Delay token.
- If VIP is Eliminated, Defender awards +2 VP immediately.
- Sanctuary: Defender's deployment edge is an 8 MU deep Sanctuary Zone. Models within Sanctuary receive +1 Wild die Defender Tests but cannot perform Attack actions. Each full Turn Defender maintains at least 25 percent of BP within Sanctuary awards +1 VP at End of Turn. Defender wins immediately upon accumulating Small 3 VP, Medium 4 VP, Large 5 VP.
- Catalyst: At End of Turn 2 (Small) / Turn 3 (Medium) / Turn 4 (Large), Defender places one d6 as Reinforcement die. At Start of next Turn, roll die. On 4+, Defender receives reinforcement:
  - Small: +75 BP deployed within 6 MU of edge, arrives Distracted.
  - Medium: +150 BP deployed within 8 MU of edge, arrives Distracted.
  - Large: +250 BP deployed within 10 MU of edge, arrives Distracted.
- If VIP reaches Extraction Zone before Catalyst trigger Turn ends, Catalyst is cancelled.

**Game Length**
- Use the standard game length rules.

**Keys to Victory**
- Courier: Immediate Attacker victory upon VIP survival threshold.
- Sanctuary: Immediate Defender victory upon VP threshold.
- VIP Elimination: +2 VP to Defender (may trigger Sanctuary victory).
- Dominance: +1 VP per Turn controlling battlefield center (4 MU radius circle).

## QAI Mission 17: Triumvirate

Three factions converge on a derelict facility containing three power nodes. Control the nodes to unlock the central vault, but alliances shift with every Turn.

**Pre-game**
Use the standard rules. There are 3 mutually Opposing Sides.

**Models per Side / BP / Battlefield / End-game**
- Small: 4 to 8, 250 to 500 BP, 24 x 24 MU, Turn 4
- Medium: 6 to 12, 500 to 750 BP, 36 x 36 MU, Turn 6
- Large: 8 to 16, 750 to 1000 BP, 48 x 48 MU, Turn 8

**Battlefield**
- Terrain.
- Set up the Battlefield using the rules for Environments and Terrain.
- Place three Control Points (PNs), each a POI terrain piece:
  - Small: 8 MU apart in triangle formation centered on battlefield.
  - Medium: 12 MU apart in triangle formation centered on battlefield.
  - Large: 16 MU apart in triangle formation centered on battlefield.
- Each PN must have at least one terrain element providing Cover within 4 MU.
- No terrain within 4 MU of battlefield center (Central Vault zone).
- Divide battlefield perimeter into three equal Entry Edges (one per Side).

**Deployment**
- Roll 1d6 to determine deployment order (1-2: Side A, 3-4: Side B, 5-6: Side C).
- Each Side deploys within 6 MU of their assigned Entry Edge.
- No model may deploy within 6 MU of any Power Node.
- No Hidden starts.

**Special Rules**
- Dominance: At End of each Turn, each Side controlling a PN (at least 1 model within 4 MU, no Opposing models within 4 MU) awards +1 VP. First Side to accumulate 4 VP wins immediately.
- Harvest: Place Neutral VIPs (NAs) equal to game size (Small: 3, Medium: 5, Large: 7). Each NA is a Small OM placed randomly (roll 2d6 x 2/3/4 MU from corner). Acquire with 1 AP Fiddle action. Extract by exiting any battlefield edge (1 AP) to award +1 VP immediately. Extracting model acquires 1 Delay token.
- Commander: Each Side designates one model as Commander (at least 61 BP). Commander grants one ability (chosen at deployment):
  - Tactical Insight: Once per game, spend 1 AP to grant +1 Wild die to all Friendly models within 8 MU for their next Action.
  - Field Promotion: Once per game, when Friendly model within 4 MU is KO'd, spend 2 AP to inflict 1 Wound + 1 Delay token instead.
  - Rally Cry: Once per game, spend 2 AP to remove all Delay tokens from Friendly models within 8 MU (Commander becomes Distracted).
- Eliminating an Opposing Commander awards +2 VP immediately. If Commander is Eliminated, that Side cannot gain Dominance VP on the next Turn.
- Anti-Kingmaking: A model may not target an Opposing model that is Engaged with a third Side's model unless that model attacked them this Turn. When a Side is Eliminated, all its controlled PNs become Neutral and all carried NAs drop as Neutral OMs. Each remaining Side receives +1 VP when a third Side is Eliminated before Turn 4 (Small), Turn 5 (Medium), or Turn 6 (Large).

**Game Length**
- Use the standard game length rules.

**Keys to Victory**
- Dominance: +1 VP per PN controlled per Turn, 4 VP total wins immediately.
- Harvest: +1 VP per Neutral VIP extracted.
- Commander Elimination: +2 VP immediately.
- Sanctuary: +1 VP per Turn maintaining at least 25 percent of BP within own Entry Edge zone.
- Bottled: +1 VP if Opposing Side fails Bottle Test.

## QAI Mission 18: Stealth

Infiltrate the blacksite and extract the encrypted Data Core before security protocols lock down the facility. Move unseen, or trigger the alarms that seal your fate.

**Pre-game**
Use the standard rules. There are just 2 Sides.

**Models per Side / BP / Battlefield / End-game**
- Small: 4 to 8, 250 to 500 BP, 24 x 24 MU, Turn 4
- Medium: 6 to 12, 500 to 750 BP, 36 x 36 MU, Turn 6
- Large: 8 to 16, 750 to 1000 BP, 48 x 48 MU, Turn 8

**Battlefield**
- Terrain.
- Set up the Battlefield using the rules for Environments and Terrain.
- Visibility is Twilight, Overcast: 4 MU OR for all models.
- Provide one Data Core OM. It is Small and Hidden at game start.
- Roll 2d6 for placement:
  - Small: (2d6 x 2 MU, 2d6 x 2 MU) from corner.
  - Medium: (2d6 x 3 MU, 2d6 x 3 MU) from corner.
  - Large: (2d6 x 4 MU, 2d6 x 4 MU) from corner.
- Minimum 4 MU from any battlefield edge.
- At least 75 percent battlefield coverage must be Cover-providing terrain.
- No open sightlines longer than 6 MU.

**Deployment**
- Mission Defender (Security) deploys first within 8 MU of one battlefield edge.
- All models may start in Wait status at no AP cost.
- No two models may deploy within 4 MU of each other.
- Mission Attacker (Infiltrators) deploys second on the opposite edge.
- All models not in LOS of Security models may start as Hidden.
- No model may deploy within 8 MU of any edge except their own.

**Special Rules**
- Alarm Level starts at 0. Increase Alarm Level when:
  - An Infiltrator is Revealed within 8 MU of Security deployment zone: +1.
  - An Infiltrator performs an Attack within 12 MU of Security deployment zone: +2.
  - A decoy Data Core is extracted: +2.
- Alarm effects:
  - Level 2+: Security gains +1 Base die Hit Tests.
  - Level 4+ (Small) / Level 6+ (Medium) / Level 8+ (Large): Security Visibility OR increases to 6 MU.
  - Lockdown Threshold reached: Game ends after current Turn. Security wins unless Authentic Core already extracted.
- Data Core is revealed when first model comes within 4 MU. Immediately roll 1d6:
  - 1-3: Authentic Core, extraction awards +3 VP and immediate Attacker victory.
  - 4-6: Decoy Core, extraction awards +1 VP but triggers +2 Alarm Level.
- Acquire Core: 1 AP Fiddle action while Free and in base-contact.
- Extract Core: 1 AP while in base-contact with Attacker's deployment edge. Model carrying Core loses Hidden status permanently and acquires 1 Delay token.
- One Infiltrator model may be designated Specialist (at least 30 BP). Once per game, while Hidden and Attentive Ordered within 4 MU of a Security model, spend 2 AP to perform a Close Combat attack that ignores Armor Rating and does not increase Alarm Level. If Specialist is Revealed within 8 MU of Security deployment zone, Alarm Level increases by +2 immediately.

**Game Length**
- Use the standard game length rules.

**Keys to Victory**
- Courier: +3 VP and immediate victory for extracting Authentic Core.
- Lockdown: Security victory when Alarm Level reaches threshold.
- Elimination: Security victory if all Infiltrators are Eliminated.
- Expiration: Security victory if game ends with no Authentic Core extracted.

## QAI Mission 19: Defiance

The fortress gates have fallen. Now you hold the inner sanctum against endless waves. Survive the onslaught, but static defense invites collapse. Strike out to break their momentum, or hold the line until dawn.

**Pre-game**
Use the standard rules. There are just 2 Sides.

**Models per Side / BP / Battlefield / End-game**
- Small: 4 to 8, Def 250 / Horde 375 BP, 24 x 24 MU, Turn 4
- Medium: 6 to 12, Def 500 / Horde 750 BP, 36 x 36 MU, Turn 6
- Large: 8 to 16, Def 750 / Horde 1000 BP, 48 x 48 MU, Turn 8

**Battlefield**
- Terrain.
- Set up the Battlefield using the rules for Environments and Terrain.
- Place a Sanctum Zone (circle) at battlefield center:
  - Small: 4 MU diameter.
  - Medium: 6 MU diameter.
  - Large: 8 MU diameter.
- Place one Hard Cover terrain element within the Sanctum Zone.
- Create three Approach Lanes radiating from Sanctum Zone to battlefield edges (120 degree separation):
  - Small: 4 MU wide lanes.
  - Medium: 6 MU wide lanes.
  - Large: 8 MU wide lanes.
- Each lane must contain two terrain elements creating chokepoints at 1/3 and 2/3 lane length.
- No terrain within 2 MU of Sanctum boundary.

**Deployment**
- Defenders deploy first entirely within Sanctum Zone.
- All models may start in Wait status at no AP cost.
- No two Defender models may start within 2 MU of each other.
- Horde deploys no models at game start. All Horde models begin in Reserve status.
- At Start of Turn 1, place three d6 as Reinforcement dice (one per Approach Lane).
- At Start of each Turn, roll all active Reinforcement dice. Each die showing 4+ deploys BP worth of Horde models at that lane's battlefield edge:
  - Small: 75 BP per successful die.
  - Medium: 150 BP per successful die.
  - Large: 250 BP per successful die.
- Deployed models may immediately move up to half MOV toward Sanctum.

**Special Rules**
- Last Stand: At End of each Turn, Defenders award +1 VP if at least 50 percent of starting models remain within Sanctum Zone. Defenders win if they accumulate Small 3 VP, Medium 4 VP, Large 5 VP.
- Sanctum Degradation: For each full Turn Defenders remain entirely within Sanctum Zone:
  - Small: Turn 2+: -1 Base die Hit Tests; Turn 3+: Hard Cover degrades to Soft.
  - Medium: Turn 3+: -1 Base die Hit Tests; Turn 5+: Hard Cover degrades to Soft.
  - Large: Turn 4+: -1 Base die Hit Tests; Turn 7+: Hard Cover degrades to Soft.
- Breakthrough: When Horde models touch Sanctum boundary, place markers:
  - First model: Breakthrough Alpha, all Horde gain +1 Base die Hit Tests.
  - Third model (Small) / Fifth model (Medium) / Seventh model (Large): Breakthrough Beta, Defender Wait status automatically removed next Turn.
  - Fifth model (Small) / Eighth model (Medium) / Eleventh model (Large): Breakthrough Gamma, Horde wins immediately.
- Defenders may remove a Breakthrough marker by eliminating all Horde models within 8 MU of that marker.
- Sally Forth: Defender models exiting Sanctum Zone may perform Sally Action (Complex Action, 2 AP): move up to MOV + 2 inches, first Close Combat attack gains +1 Wild die. Model cannot re-enter Sanctum this Turn. Each Horde model Eliminated by a Sallying Defender awards +1 VP immediately. Defenders outside Sanctum at End of Turn do not count toward Last Stand VP threshold.
- Horde receives +1 AP per full 6 models on battlefield (rounded down) at Start of each Turn.

**Game Length**
- Use the standard game length rules.

**Keys to Victory**
- Last Stand: Defender victory upon reaching VP threshold.
- Breakthrough: Horde victory upon placing Breakthrough Gamma marker.
- Elimination: Horde victory if all Defenders are Eliminated.

## QAI Mission 20: Breach

The vault contains three security switches. Flip them all to unlock the exit corridor, but every movement risks detection. The defenders stand vigilant in Wait status, their heightened awareness your greatest obstacle.

**Pre-game**
Use the standard rules. There are just 2 Sides.

**Models per Side / BP / Battlefield / End-game**
- Small: 4 to 8, 250 to 500 BP, 24 x 24 MU, Turn 4
- Medium: 6 to 12, 500 to 750 BP, 36 x 36 MU, Turn 6
- Large: 8 to 16, 750 to 1000 BP, 48 x 48 MU, Turn 8

**Battlefield**
- Terrain.
- Set up the Battlefield using the rules for Environments and Terrain.
- Visibility is Twilight, Overcast: 8 MU OR for all models.
- Place three Access Switches (POI terrain pieces) forming an equilateral triangle centered on battlefield:
  - Small: 8 MU from center point.
  - Medium: 12 MU from center point.
  - Large: 16 MU from center point.
- No terrain within 4 MU of any Switch.
- Each Switch must have one Hard Cover element within 2 MU.
- Place Exit Corridor (6 MU wide strip) along one battlefield edge opposite Infiltrator deployment edge. It remains closed until all three Switches are On.

**Deployment**
- Mission Defender (Sentinels) deploys first within 8 MU of Exit Corridor edge.
- All models begin in Wait status at no AP cost.
- No two models may deploy within 6 MU of each other.
- Mission Attacker (Infiltrators) deploys second on opposite battlefield edge.
- All models not in LOS of Sentinels may start as Hidden.
- No model may deploy within 8 MU of any Switch.

**Special Rules**
- Switch Activation: A Free model in base-contact with a Switch spends 2 AP Fiddle action to toggle its state (Off or On). Model performing activation acquires 1 Delay token and cannot use Wait status this Turn.
- Exit Corridor opens immediately when all three Switches are On simultaneously at End of Turn. Once open, Infiltrators may extract: 1 AP while in base-contact with Exit Corridor edge removes model from play and awards +1 VP. Infiltrators win immediately upon extracting Small 3 models, Medium 4 models, Large 5 models.
- Wait Status: Sentinels in Wait status have doubled Visibility OR (16 MU). Hidden Infiltrators without Cover in LOS of Wait models are immediately Revealed. Wait status maintained at no AP cost if Attentive Free at start of Initiative. Involuntary Delay tokens force Wait removal at next Initiative.
- Dominance: At End of each Turn:
  - Infiltrators receive +1 VP if exactly 2 Switches are On.
  - Infiltrators receive +2 VP if all 3 Switches are On (Exit opens).
  - Sentinels receive +1 VP if at least 2 models in Wait status within 8 MU of any Switch and no Infiltrators within 4 MU of that Switch.
- Sentinels win immediately upon accumulating Small 3 VP, Medium 4 VP, Large 5 VP.
- Reposition: Any model may perform Reposition (Simple Action, 1 AP): move up to MOV x 1 inch without terrain penalties, cannot end in base-contact with Opposing models, cannot trigger Reacts. Does not break Wait status if model remains Attentive Free.

**Game Length**
- Use the standard game length rules.

**Keys to Victory**
- Courier: Immediate victory for extracting required models after Exit opens.
- Dominance: Sentinel victory upon reaching VP threshold; Infiltrator VP from Switch control.
- Elimination: +1 VP to Side with most BP eliminated (tiebreaker only).

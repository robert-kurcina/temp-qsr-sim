# Mission Keys

## Missions and Scenarios

Missions define starting, end, and victory conditions. Scenarios are missions that also specify terrain and assemblies to be used.

## Mission Elements

Common mission elements include battlefield edges, points of interest, VIPs, resource points, objective markers, and keys to victory.

### Battlefield Edges, Zones, and Corners

- **Edges:** Deployment edges for each side.
- **Zones:** Each edge has a zone running the length of the battlefield, with a width of 2, 4, or 8 MU for Small, Medium, or Large games.
- **Two Sides:** Opposing sides deploy on opposite edges.
- **Three or Four Sides:** Each side picks an edge. Each edge stops before corners by 2, 4, or 8 MU for Small, Medium, or Large games.
- **Corners:** Some missions define corner zones with a radius of 4, 8, or 16 MU for Small, Medium, or Large games.

### Points of Interest (POI)

A POI is a large terrain piece about 12" x 12" placed at battlefield center, such as a building or hill.

- Traversable and may have gates or doors on each side.
- Entrances are initially closed and require a Fiddle action to open or close.
- Control ties to Dominance (turn-based) and POI (end-game) keys.

### VIP (Very-Important-Person)

VIPs are characters that appear on the battlefield.

- **Archetype:** Untrained, Scholarly archetype unless specified. No items unless specified.
- **Control:** A player controls the VIP when a Friendly Attentive Ordered model is in base-contact and no Ordered Opposing models are within Cohesion. Control is a 2 AP Fiddle action. If a Guardian starts in base-contact, the VIP maintains base-contact.
- **Behavior:** If not in base-contact with a Guardian, the VIP moves toward Cover or toward the Guardian if it has LOS. KO or Eliminated Guardians break control.
- **Transfer:** Control can be transferred to another Friendly model within Cohesion if all involved are Attentive. Transfer is a 1 AP Fiddle action.
- **Abilities:** The VIP has one ability chosen at deployment, usable once per game when Attentive Ordered within 8 MU of an objective.

### Resource Points

Resource Points (RP) are a secondary scoring layer to award extra VP and break ties.

- +1 VP to the Side with the most RP.
- If the top RP total is double the next highest and at least 10 RP greater, award +2 VP instead.
- If VP are tied, the Side with most RP wins.

### Objective Markers (OMs)

Objective Markers are described per mission and represent items or objectives on the battlefield.

- **Types:** Switches, Locks, Keys, Ideas, Tiny, Small, Large, Bulky.
- **Switches:** Toggle state; do not move; On/Off.
- **Locks:** Switches that require paired Ideas or Physical OMs.
- **Keys:** Idea or Physical OMs paired with a Lock.
- **Ideas:** Intangible OMs; no limit to how many can be carried.
- **Tiny:** Default OM type; requires at least 2 AP to acquire.
- **Small:** Physical OM assigned `[1H]` and `[Stub]`, about loaf-sized.
- **Large:** Physical OM assigned `[1H]`. Two Large OMs grant `[Laden]`.
- **Bulky:** Physical OM assigned `[2H]` and `[Laden 2]`, about 10 kg.

#### OM Actions

- **Acquire:** While Free and no Attentive Opposing models are also in base-contact, acquire with a 1 AP Fiddle action. Switch OMs stay in place; others are carried.
- **Transfer:** While in base-contact, transfer Physical OMs with a 1 AP Fiddle action if at least one is Attentive Ordered. If Stunned, Disordered, or Distracted, transfer costs 2 AP. Physical OMs transferred from Opposing models become Neutral.
- **Shared:** Idea OMs require 1 AP plus one per total Hindrance between two Free Friendly Attentive characters within Cohesion. Both gain a copy, but only one copy benefits scoring.

#### OM Concerns

- **Scoring:** Each OM acquired awards +1 RP. Switch OMs are acquired when first toggled. Physical OMs carried by Eliminated characters do not score.
- **Dropping:** KO'd or Eliminated characters drop all Physical OMs at their location. Dropped OMs become Neutral.
- **Improvised Weapons:** Physical OMs can be used as Improvised weapons, but break and become worthless when used in a Damage test.
- **Destroying:** Switch OMs cannot be attacked or destroyed unless a mission specifies it. Physical OMs can be destroyed with a 1 AP Fiddle action. Idea OMs are usually destroyed when the owner is Eliminated.

## Keys to Victory

Keys are common victory conditions. Missions list which keys apply.

- **Acquisition:** +1 VP to Mission Attacker for taking one or more Objective Markers off the battlefield.
- **Aggression:** +1 VP if at least half of the Assembly's models cross an imaginary mid-line. +1 RP to the first model to cross.
- **VIP:** +1 VP to Side protecting the VIP, +2 VP to Opponent if Eliminated. VIP may use a special ability once per game when Attentive Ordered within 8 MU of an objective.
- **Bottled:** +1 VP if Opposing Side fails its Bottle Test or has no Ordered characters. In 3+ Side games with at least two Opposing Sides remaining, award those sides +3 RP instead each time a side Bottles Out.
- **Catalyst:** +1 VP to Side that first triggers a specified battlefield condition or event. Trigger via 2 AP Fiddle action, then modify battlefield state as specified.
- **Collection:** +1 VP to player with most OMs acquired. Each OM acquired awards +1 RP at game end.
- **Courier:** +1 VP if a model makes base-contact with a designated location. Awards +1 VP immediately, then +1 VP per subsequent Turn it remains within 4 MU. Model may leave to reset delivery elsewhere.
- **Dominance:** +1 VP per Turn a Side maintains exclusive control of designated zones or objectives. VP accrues at End of Turn.
- **Elimination:** +1 VP if a Side has the most Opposing KO'd and Eliminated characters by BP total at game end.
- **Encroachment:** +1 VP to be the first Side with a model to cross the mid-point line toward an Opposing Assembly.
- **Exit:** +1 VP to Mission Defender if at least half of its original assembly exits the battlefield at designated areas.
- **First Blood:** +1 VP to the first Side to Wound an Opposing model, or if in Range Combat to KO or Eliminate it.
- **Flawless:** Automatic victory to the last player with a character not Eliminated nor KO'd at game end.
- **Outnumbered:** +1 VP to Side outnumbered 3:2 or greater at game start, +2 VP if outnumbered 2:1 or greater. If multiple Sides remain at game end, do not award this key.
- **POI:** +1 VP to Side controlling the majority of POIs at game end. Control requires a Friendly character within 4 MU of the POI center and no Opposing models within Cohesion of that character.
- **Sanctuary:** +1 VP per Turn a Side maintains a specified percentage of BP within designated deployment zones. Models in Sanctuary gain +1 Wild die Defender Tests but cannot perform Attack actions.
- **Targeted:** +1 VP when a specified character or model is KO'd or Eliminated. By default, this is the highest BP character at mission start. +1 RP per Officer Rank and +1 RP per Reputation.

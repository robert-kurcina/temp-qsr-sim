## QSR Tech Level Restrictions

**For Quick Start Rules (QSR) games:**

**Default Tech Level:** 1-3 (Ancient: Stone to Iron)
**Extended QSR:** Tech Levels 1-5 (Ancient to Medieval)

**Recommended for Learning:**
- **Tech Level 1-2:** Bronze Age fantasy (swords, spears, bows)
- **Tech Level 3:** Iron Age (mail armor, crossbows)
- **Tech Level 5:** Medieval (plate armor, polearms)

## Item Availability by Tech Level

### Ancient Period (Tech 1-3)

**Tech 1 (Stone Age):**
- **Weapons:** Clubs, axes, spears, daggers, bows, slings
- **Armor:** Hide armor, basic helmets, small shields
- **Equipment:** Torches, basic medicinals, hooks

**Tech 2 (Bronze Age):**
- **Adds:** Battle axes, long axes, war boomerangs, chakrams
- **Special:** Leadership notebooks, tactics notebooks

**Tech 3 (Iron Age):**
- **Adds:** Mail armor, crossbows, ballista, reinforced weapons
- **Special:** Armored gear (bracers, padding)

### Archaic Period (Tech 5-7)

**Tech 5 (Medieval):**
- **Adds:** Great swords, scimitars, heavy crossbows
- **Firearms:** Archaic rifles (early gunpowder)
- **Explosives:** Basic bombs

**Tech 6 (Renaissance):**
- **Adds:** Rapiers, powderkegs
- **Firearms:** Early pistols

**Tech 7 (Colonial):**
- **Adds:** Bayonets, muskets, arquebus
- **Explosives:** Petards

### Expansionist Period (Tech 8-10)

**Tech 8 (Sail):**
- **Adds:** Cutlasses, blunderbuss, spyglasses
- **Equipment:** Goggles, lanterns

**Tech 9 (Industrial):**
- **Adds:** Sabers, ammo boxes, detonators

**Tech 10 (Machine):**
- **Adds:** Revolvers, gatling guns, cannons
- **Armor:** Springsuits
- **Equipment:** Electric lanterns

### Modern Period (Tech 11-13)

**Tech 11 (Modern):**
- **Weapons:** Auto rifles, SMGs, shotguns, grenades
- **Armor:** Bulletproof vests, hardsuits
- **Equipment:** Gas masks, medical kits, targeting systems

**Tech 12 (Atomic):**
- **Adds:** Heavy machine guns, mortars, auto-cannons
- **Equipment:** Night-vision goggles, spotlights

**Tech 13 (Information):**
- **Adds:** Rail-guns, advanced sniper scopes
- **Equipment:** Advanced comm-links

### Near Period (Tech 14-15)

**Tech 14 (Robotics):**
- **Weapons:** Coil-guns, sonic weapons, needlers
- **Armor:** Nanoweave armor, exo-skeletons
- **Equipment:** Jet-packs, sensor arrays, automedics

**Tech 15 (Fusion):**
- **Weapons:** Plasma weapons, beam rifles, bolters
- **Armor:** Powered armor, plasmashields
- **Equipment:** Fusion power cells

### Far Period (Tech 16-18)

**Tech 16 (Quantum):**
- **Weapons:** Vibroblades, beam pistols
- **Armor:** Combat powered armor

**Tech 17 (Energy):**
- **Weapons:** Arc weapons, blast cannons, blasters
- **Armor:** Stealth powered armor, battle armor

**Tech 18 (Gravity):**
- **Equipment:** Anti-gravity harnesses, displacer devices

### Fantastic Period (Tech 19-20)

**Tech 19 (Symbolic - Low Magic):**
- **Arcanum:** Amulets, rings, scrolls, tomes
- **Equipment:** Mask of Command, belts of strength

**Tech 20 (Symbolic - High Magic):**
- **Arcanum:** Spectral weapons, teleporters
- **Equipment:** Rings of flight, amulets of power

### ANY Period (Universal)

**Upgrades & Packages:**
- **Upgrades:** Balanced, Masterwork, Poisoned, Reinforced, Well-honed
- **Packages:** Assassin, Berserker, Recon, Warrior, Timer
- **Special:** Heirloom Weapon, Favored Passage, Immune-Blast

## Genre Settings

### Bronze Age Fantasy
**Tech Levels:** 1-2
**Available Items:** Basic weapons, hide/bronze armor, bows
**Typical Assemblies:** Warriors with spears/shields, archers, champions

### Medieval Fantasy
**Tech Levels:** 3-5
**Available Items:** Mail/plate armor, swords, polearms, crossbows
**Typical Assemblies:** Knights, men-at-arms, longbowmen

### Renaissance
**Tech Levels:** 5-7
**Available Items:** Plate armor, rapiers, early firearms
**Typical Assemblies:** Musketeers, duelists, pikemen

### Modern Military
**Tech Levels:** 11-13
**Available Items:** Assault rifles, body armor, grenades
**Typical Assemblies:** Infantry squads, special forces

### Science Fiction
**Tech Levels:** 14-18
**Available Items:** Energy weapons, powered armor, jet-packs
**Typical Assemblies:** Space marines, scouts, heavy troopers

### Fantasy High Magic
**Tech Levels:** 1-5 + 19-20
**Available Items:** Medieval weapons + magical arcanum
**Typical Assemblies:** Knights, mages, magical beasts

### Mixed Tech (Recommended for Learning)
**Tech Levels:** 1-5 (Ancient to Medieval)
**Available Items:** All melee weapons, bows, crossbows, basic armor
**Note:** This is the **default QSR configuration**

## Using Tech Levels in Games

### Step 1: Agree on Tech Level

Before building Assemblies, all players should agree on:
- **Tech Period** (Ancient, Modern, Sci-Fi, etc.)
- **Tech Level** (specific number within period)
- **Genre** (fantasy, historical, sci-fi, etc.)

### Step 2: Filter Available Items

Only items available at the agreed Tech Level or lower are available for purchase.

**Example:**
```
Agreed: Tech Level 3 (Iron Age)
Available: All Tech 1, 2, and 3 items
Unavailable: Tech 5+ (Medieval firearms and later)
```

### Step 3: Build Assemblies

Build Assemblies using only available items within BP budget.

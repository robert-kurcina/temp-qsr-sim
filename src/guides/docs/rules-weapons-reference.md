- **Sword, (Broad)** is commonly referred to as just "Sword"

**Notes:**
- These represent magical or energy-based ranged weapons
- **Class: Range** indicates general ranged weapon (not Bow, Thrown, etc.)

## Weapon Selection by Genre

### Bronze Age (~2250 BCE)
**Available:** Club, Daggers, Spear, Bow Medium, Axe
**Typical Loadouts:**
- Spearman: Spear + Dagger (36 BP)
- Archer: Bow Medium + Dagger (13 BP)
- Warrior: Axe + Club (19 BP)

### Medieval (~500-1500 CE)
**Available:** All melee weapons, Bow Medium, Bow Long, Crossbow Medium
**Typical Loadouts:**
- Knight: Sword Broad + Spear (47 BP)
- Archer: Bow Long + Sword Saber (26 BP)
- Crossbowman: Crossbow Medium + Dagger (16 BP)

### Renaissance (~1300-1700 CE)
**Available:** All weapons except magical
**Typical Loadouts:**
- Duelist: Sword Saber + Dagger (19 BP)
- Pikeman: Spear + Sword Saber (43 BP)
- Arquebusier: (Use Crossbow stats) + Sword (use Saber)

### Modern (~1900-2025 CE)
**Available:** Improvised weapons, Throwing Knives
**Typical Loadouts:**
- Soldier: Improvised Melee (rifle as club) + Throwing Knives
- Civilian: Improvised Melee + Improvised Thrown

### Science Fiction / Fantasy (~2025+)
**Available:** All weapons including Magical Ranged
**Typical Loadouts:**
- Mage: Blast Minor + Dagger (36 BP)
- Soldier: Blast Major + Sword (70 BP)
- Scout: Bow Long + Blast Minor (43 BP)

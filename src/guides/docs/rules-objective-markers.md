# Objective Markers

Objective Markers (OMs) are mission-specific objectives. Missions describe their types, counts, and placement. OMs are typically placed near Obstacles to indicate important locations.

## OM Types

An OM may include one or more of the following types:

- **Switches:** Switch OMs toggle game elements. They do not move and cannot be collected. Each has an On and Off state.
- **Lock:** A Switch OM that can only be toggled if the paired Idea or Physical OMs are In Hand.
- **Key:** An Idea or Physical OM paired with a specifically named Lock OM.
- **Idea:** An abstract OM (knowledge or information). No limit to how many can be In Hand.
- **Tiny:** Default Physical OM. Tiny OMs require at least 2 AP to acquire.
- **Small:** Physical OM assigned `[1H]` and `[Stub]`, about the size of a loaf of bread.
- **Large:** Physical OM assigned `[1H]`. Having two gives the set the `[Laden]` trait. About the size of a small bag of flour.
- **Bulky:** Physical OM assigned `[2H]` and `[Laden 2]` traits. About 10 kg in mass or the size of a large bag of dog food.

## Actions

OMs may be acquired, transferred, and shared.

### Acquire

- While Free, if no Attentive Opposing models are also in base-contact, acquire with a 1 AP Fiddle action.
- Unless the OM is a Switch, it is carried and considered In Hand.
- Animals cannot acquire Idea OMs, but may alter Switch OMs.
- Characters normally have 2 Hands, and can use an additional Hand at a penalty of -1 Base die for all Combat Tests and Fiddle Tests.

### Transfer

- While in base-contact, Physical OMs can be transferred with a 1 AP Fiddle action between Friendly characters if at least one is Attentive Ordered.
- If the transfer involves Stunned or Disordered Distracted characters, it costs 2 AP. This includes Opposing characters.
- Physical OMs transferred from Opposing models are treated as Neutral OMs.

### Shared

- Idea OMs require 1 AP, plus one per total Hindrance between two Free Friendly Attentive characters within Cohesion.
- Both characters gain a copy, but only one copy counts for scoring.

## Concerns

- **Scoring:** Each OM acquired awards +1 RP. Switch OMs score when first toggled. Physical OMs are not scored if the carrier is Eliminated.
- **Dropping:** KO'd or Eliminated characters drop Physical OMs at their location. Dropped OMs become Neutral and can be claimed by any Side.
- **Improvised Weapons:** Physical OMs can be used as Improvised weapons but break and become worthless when used in a Damage test.
- **Destroying:** Switch OMs cannot be attacked or destroyed unless specified by a mission. Physical OMs can be destroyed with a 1 AP Fiddle action. Idea OMs are usually destroyed when the owner is Eliminated.

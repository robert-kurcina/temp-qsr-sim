Attacking KO’d Models ()
Attacking KO’d characters is disallowed for the Basic Game
and Introductory Game for when learning how to play. This
rule prevents anti-thematic attacks on most KO’d
characters.
 Characters with certain classifications, as shown here
with keywords, adjust these rules as follows:
 Panicked — Characters which are Panicked may always
perform attacks against Opposing KO’d models.
 Amoral — Characters which are “evil” or “dishonorable”
may attack KO’d characters. These attackers must have
one or more of the following traits; [Delusional],
[Coward], [Ravenous], or [Treacherous], [Vitriol], Fear,
Insane, Poisoner, Terrifying, or Torment.
 Primal — Animal characters which have any of the
[Beast], [Beast!], or [Beast+] traits are never allowed to
attack KO’d models unless it also has one of these traits;
[Ravenous], Fear, Insane, or Terrifying traits.
 Puppet — Characters with the Automaton or [Mindless]
trait may attack KO’d models if their Controller or
Coordinator character has one or more of these traits;
[Delusional], [Coward], [Treacherous], Insane.
 Unnatural — Targets which have any of the following
Traits may always be attacked if they are KO’d;
[Automaton], Invader, Outsider, Supernatural, Mythos,
Fear, Machine, [Mindless], or [Ravenous].
 When KO’d models are attacked, do the following:
 KO’d models may be attacked in order to cause the target
to become Eliminated.
 KO’d models are attacked via an Unopposed RCA Test and
award +3 Attacker Range Combat Hit Test.
 KO’d targets at Melee Range are automatically hit; merely
perform the Damage Test with +3 Damage.
 KO’d models reduce each Armor Rating by 3.
 Causing a Wound damage, but at least the target’s SIZ
minus 3 or greater will Eliminate the model. Otherwise,
nothing happens.
